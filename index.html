Features

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

WhatsApp Broadcasting

Connect once via QR scan. Murmur uses your existing WhatsApp session to send — no API keys, no fees, no ban risk from bulk APIs.

Gmail Broadcasting

Add your Gmail App Password and send rich personalized emails. Full subject + body support with {{variable}} substitution.

CSV Contact Import

Upload any CSV with name, phone, email, tag, birthday columns. Extra columns become {{variables}} automatically. Filter by group or hand-pick contacts.

Scheduling

Send now, schedule for later, or set up recurring sends — daily, weekly, monthly, or on birthdays. Runs silently from the system tray.

Live Logs & Retry

Track every send in real time — delivered, failed, pending. One-click retry for failed messages. Full send history with timestamps.

100% Local & Private

SQLite database on your machine. No external servers. No analytics. No telemetry. Your contacts, messages, and credentials never leave your device.

How it works

Up and running in minutes

1

Connect your accounts

Scan a QR code to link your WhatsApp, and enter your Gmail App Password. Both stay on your machine — Murmur never sees your credentials.

2

Import contacts from CSV

Upload a .csv file with your contacts. Murmur reads name, phone, email, tag, and any extra columns you add as template variables.

3

Compose your message

Write your message using {{name}}, {{tag}}, or any custom column as a variable. Preview exactly what each recipient will receive.

4

Schedule and send

Send immediately, schedule for a specific time, or set up a recurring broadcast. Watch live progress or check back in the logs later.

CSV Format

Your CSV must have at least a name column. Add others as needed:

name,phone,email,tag,birthday,company
Ahmed Khan,+923001234567,ahmed@gmail.com,client,1990-03-15,Acme Corp
Sara Ali,+923009876543,sara@email.com,family,1988-07-22,
  • name — required
  • phone — required for WhatsApp (+country code format)
  • email — required for Gmail
  • tag — optional, used to filter/group contacts
  • birthday — optional, enables birthday scheduling (YYYY-MM-DD)
  • Any extra column (e.g. company) becomes a {{variable}}

Message Variables

Use {{double braces}} in your message body or subject to personalize for each recipient:

Hi {{name}},

Just wanted to reach out to you
at {{company}}. Hope you're doing well!

Best,
Your Name
  • {{name}} — contact's name
  • {{email}} — contact's email
  • {{tag}} — contact's group/tag
  • {{birthday}} — contact's birthday
  • {{column_name}} — any custom CSV column

Gmail Setup

Murmur uses a Gmail App Password — not your real password. This is safer and works even with 2FA.

  1. Go to myaccount.google.com
  2. Navigate to Security → 2-Step Verification and enable it
  3. Then go to Security → App Passwords
  4. Create a new App Password for "Mail"
  5. Paste the 16-character password into Murmur

WhatsApp Setup

Murmur connects to WhatsApp Web using your phone — the same way the browser version works.

  1. Click Connect WhatsApp in Murmur
  2. Open WhatsApp on your phone
  3. Tap Linked Devices → Link a Device
  4. Scan the QR code shown in Murmur
  5. You're connected — the session persists across restarts

⚠️ Requires Google Chrome to be installed on your machine.

Scheduling Options

  • Send Now — fires immediately

  • Weekly — repeats on the same day each week

FAQ

Common questions

Is this safe to use? Will my WhatsApp get banned?

Murmur uses the same technology as WhatsApp Web — your number connects via a QR scan. The key risk factor is sending too fast. Murmur enforces a minimum delay between messages (default 5 seconds) to mimic human behaviour. Avoid sending to thousands of people at once.

Where is my data stored?

Everything — contacts, credentials, logs, message history — is stored in a local SQLite database on your machine (~/Library/Application Support/Murmur/murmur.db on Mac, %APPDATA%/Murmur on Windows). Nothing is ever sent to a server.

Does it work when my computer is off / asleep?

No. Murmur runs locally — if your machine is off or the app is closed, scheduled sends won't fire. Keep the app running in the system tray for scheduled broadcasts.

Can I send to thousands of contacts?

Technically yes, but WhatsApp has rate limits. For large lists, use a generous delay (10s+) and split into multiple smaller broadcasts. For Gmail, Google's free tier allows ~500 emails/day.

Do I need to pay for anything?

No. Murmur is MIT-licensed open source software. It's free forever. No subscriptions, no in-app purchases, no usage limits imposed by Murmur itself.

Can I contribute or request features?

Yes! Open an issue or pull request on GitHub. See CONTRIBUTING.md for guidelines.
